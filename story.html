My Story

Isha Kunwar

Buckle up now.

In the lap of Uttarakhand's mesmerizing hills, amidst the harmonious symphony of nature, I took my first breath and embarked on a journey unlike any other. Let me share with you the most captivating aspect of my life - being born and raised in this picturesque haven. It's this profound connection to my Pahadi roots that shields me from the stress and darkness of the ever-expanding urban jungle. There's something truly magical, funkier, and simpler about embracing the Pahadi way of life. Oh, how I long for the comforting embrace of my home, but alas, the relentless cycle of industrialization and development pulls us away. The demands for 'roti, kapda, and makan' are undeniably high in this bustling world. Yet, even in the distance, I find solace in being the greatest admirer of my beloved home. It's as if the hills themselves whisper tales of resilience and tranquility, reminding me that no matter where life takes me, the spirit of being a Pahadi will forever reside in my heart. With each passing day, my connection to Uttarakhand grows stronger, carrying the rich heritage and untamed beauty of the hills within me.
Now Let's discuss some attributes of my life that revolve around what I do, rather than providing a general overview of who I am. I am deeply interested in coding and designing, and I find immense satisfaction in creating things , solving problems, Traditional painting is a creative outlet that I cherish, as it allows me to express myself artistically. Moreover, I hold strong values as a civilizational enthusiast, appreciating the progress and achievements of humanity throughout history. and reading "pheww.. yes, I like all of that.. ohkay I might be lying about the reading part." While I may have exaggerated my enthusiasm for reading earlier, I still value the exploration of knowledge through various mediums. Occasionally, my mind delves into the realm of politics. It's a hunger to stay up-to-date with current events and to understand what has happened and what may transpire in the future. Maybe it's because of my father's influence, as he serves in the army, instilling in me a disciplined interest in these matters from an early age. As a result, I find myself occasionally drawn to the intricacies of Indian politics, including the amusing nuances of Uttarakhand's land laws. However, one might wonder, why bother with politics at all? Well, it's true that being knowledgeable about one's nation and its politics is valuable. Yet, there are times when it feels like a burden to possess this knowledge without the ability to directly address the problems at hand. In such instances, my way of finding solace is by striving to be a citizen with integrity. I focus on maintaining my own values and acting responsibly within my sphere of influence, even if the grand scale of politics feels beyond my control. After all, true change often begins with individual actions. Allow me to share a few facts about myself. Firstly, I have an inherent knack for noticing art in various forms. It's something that always captures my attention and brings me joy. I also consider myself somewhat introverted, although I'm not entirely sure why. There's a part of me that hesitates to open up, as if something within me retreats. Nonetheless, I've managed to navigate my college life successfully, and here I am, entering my fourth year with a sense of happiness and accomplishment. Interestingly, I've learned to overcome the initial hurdle of introducing myself to new people, especially knowing that our paths may never cross again. Thanks to my father's career in the military and the frequent transfers we experienced, I received valuable training in adapting to new environments and developing social skills. It has allowed me to become more comfortable in social situations, even as an introvert. When it comes to my favorite songs, it's difficult for me to pick just one as the best. However, lately, I've been listening to "Safar" by Reeshab Purohit. It's a soothing song that brings peace of mind whenever I listen to it. On a related note, I would love it if our conversation touched upon Pahadi music. Although I enjoy listening to songs in various languages, I'm particularly drawn to the charm and uniqueness of Pahadi music. I'm one of those individuals who actively seeks out niche songs, disregarding language barriers. Additionally, photography holds a special place in my heart. I am immensely grateful to witness the beauty of the world around me and perceive the harmonious blend of both serene and chaotic elements. Whenever I have a camera in my hand, I never hesitate to capture those fleeting moments in time. It's a way for me to appreciate and immortalize the wonders that surround us. Now, assuming you're back after listening to the audio, let's move on If you were to select a spirit animal, which one do you feel resonates with you the most? For me, it's undoubtedly the rabbit. I resonate with their curious nature and their ability to navigate through different environments with agility and grace. Speaking of curiosity, it's a trait that defines a significant part of who I am. I have an inherent desire to explore and learn about the world around me, always seeking new knowledge and experiences. There's a quote that has resonated with me deeply: " Coding is art. Community building is art. Animated loops are art. Spoken poetry is art. We don't get to define art; we just get to witness it." I can't recall the exact source of this quote, but it highlights the notion that various forms of creativity and expression are indeed artistic endeavors. It serves as a reminder that art is not confined to traditional definitions but rather emerges from the beauty and innovation we encounter in our everyday lives. Ultimately, everything I've shared above reflects my sincere endeavor to not only express myself but also gain a deeper understanding of who I am. By delving into various facets of my life, interests, and perspectives, I aim to forge a stronger connection with my own identity. This ongoing journey of exploration and sharing allows me to grow, evolve, and wholeheartedly embrace the distinctive qualities that shape my true self.
