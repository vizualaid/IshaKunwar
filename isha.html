Who am I.

हिं
In the serene embrace of Uttarakhand's majestic hills, I came into existence, greeted by the symphony of nature that surrounded me. My journey began, and it has been unlike any other. Allow me to share with you the most enchanting aspect of my life: being born and raised in this heavenly abode. The bond I share with my Pahadi roots runs deep, shielding me from the stress and shadows of the ever-expanding urban jungle. There is an undeniable magic, an inherent simplicity, in embracing the Pahadi way of life.
Oh, how my heart longs for the comforting embrace of my home! Still, I find solace as the greatest admirer of my beloved haven. The hills whisper tales of resilience, reminding me that the spirit of being a Pahadi resides forever in my heart, teaching me the true essence of life. I'm Greatfull to witnessed the awe-inspiring grandeur of snow-capped peaks, the gentle melody of flowing rivers, and the vibrant hues of blooming flowers. These experiences have shaped my perspective, teaching me the importance of preserving and cherishing our natural heritage. For no matter where my journey leads, the eternal Blessings of devbhumi will always accompany me, guiding me and keeping me close to my roots and reminding me of the true essence of life itself.
